Content Policy

By accepting the policy, these terms will automatically apply to you – you should make sure therefore that you read them carefully before using the app. Our policies are designed to ensure that our community stays protected. They set out what's allowed and not allowed on Viral App, and apply to all types of content on our platform, including webistes, videos, app installs and other content. The safety of our creators, viewers, and partners is our highest priority. We look to each of you to help us protect this unique and vibrant community. It’s important you understand our Policies, and the role they play in our shared responsibility to keep Viral App safe.

Viral App doesn’t allow anything that artificially completes campaign either by using automatic systems or serving to unsuspecting users. Also, content that solely exists to incentivize for engagement is prohibited.

Content intended to impersonate a person or online content is not allowed on Viral App. Viral App also enforces trademark holder rights. When a channel, or content in the channel, causes confusion about the source of goods and services advertised, it may not be allowed.

Links that send users to websites featuring content that violates our Policies are not allowed on Viral App.

Don’t post links in your content on Viral App if they fit any of the descriptions noted below.

  • Links to pornography
  • Links to websites or apps that install malware
  • Links to websites or apps phishing for a user’s login credentials, financial information, etc.
  • Links to websites, apps, or other information technology that give unauthorized free access to audio content, audiovisual content, full video games, software, or streaming services that normally require payment
  • Links to websites that seek to raise funds or recruit for terrorist organizations
  • Links to sites containing Child Sexual Abuse Imagery (CSAI)

Explicit content meant to be sexually gratifying is not allowed on Viral App. Posting pornography may result in content removal or account termination. Campaign containing fetish content will be removed. In most cases, violent, graphic, or humiliating fetishes are not allowed on Viral App.

Viral App doesn’t allow spam, scams, or other deceptive practices that take advantage of the Viral App community. We also don’t allow content where the main purpose is to trick others into leaving Viral App for another site.

At Viral App, we take the health and well-being of all our creators and viewers seriously. Awareness and understanding of mental health is important. We do not, however, allow content on Viral App that promotes suicide, self-harm, or is intended to shock or disgust users.

Viral App doesn’t allow content that encourages dangerous or illegal activities that risk serious physical harm or death.

Hate speech is not allowed on Viral App. We remove content promoting violence or hatred against individuals or groups.

If you find content that violates this policy, report it.

What happens if content violates this policy

If your content violates this policy, we’ll remove the content and send you an email to let you know. We’ll issue a notice against your account. Accounts and campaigns that don't follow this policy may be terminated and removed from Viral App.

Changes to This Content Policy

We may update our Content Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Content Policy on this page.
